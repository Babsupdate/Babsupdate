### The Engineer Behind ZyndrHQ

I'm **0X BABS** — a self-taught full stack engineer and Web3 architect from **Osun State, Nigeria**, and the sole founder of **ZyndrHQ** (trading as [Just-Code-Builder](https://github.com/Just-Code-Builder)).

I design and ship **cross-platform products** across iOS, Android, and Web — from system-level architecture in **Rust and Go**, to mobile apps in **React Native, Swift, and Kotlin**, to production-grade web apps in **Next.js**. I also hold a background in **Electrical & Electronics Engineering**, giving me a hardware-level intuition for systems design.

Everything I build connects through **[Kernal.io](https://kernal.io)** — a unified Core API that gives all my products shared identity, wallet, KYC verification, and push notification infrastructure. Think of it as Africa's answer to Stripe for identity + payments.

My current portfolio spans **payments, housing, commerce, social, VPN infrastructure, and engagement** — all targeting the 1.4 billion people across Africa who are chronically underserved by existing platforms.

> *"I'm not just building apps — I'm building the digital infrastructure that Africa's next generation runs on."*
